Motivation

The "Encounters in Econometric Theory" workshop aims to unite young econometricians across Europe, fostering a collaborative environment to discuss and share their research passions. Following the success of our previous editions at Nuffield College, this year marks a significant evolution as we host the event outside its traditional setting at PSE!

Our goal is to create an intimate space where we can engage with one another, exploring theoretical econometrics and the challenges that inspire us. The positive feedback from our past encounters highlights a strong desire for such interactions, which we believe are vital for establishing lasting professional relationships early in one's academic career.
